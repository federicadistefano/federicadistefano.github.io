About

I'm a PhD candidate at TU Wien since October 2020, under the supervision of Mantas Simkus and Magdalena Ortiz. My research focus is now on Description Logics, with particular emphasis on non-monotonic extensions of them. Previously, I worked on Lukasiewicz Logic. I maturated some experience as a teacher, both outside and inside academia.
