Research Interests

My research is currently focused on Non-monotonic Description Logics. More specifically, we investigate circumscription-based non-monotonic extensions of DLs with the aim of lowering the complexity (usually dangerously high) while preserving usefulness for Knowledge Representation.
My other interestes are Łukasiewicz Logic, fuzzy logics in general, and didactic for STEM disciplines.
